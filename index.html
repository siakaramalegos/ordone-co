ABOUT US

Ordone Company (Ordone Co.) is a multifaceted consulting firm headquartered in New Orleans, Louisiana. Founder, Courtney Ordone, began the company in 2013 with primary focus on private redevelopment of blighted residential and commercial properties in Post Katrina New Orleans. Ms. Ordone’s past work experience provides extensive knowledge in federal and state grant management, disaster recovery management, and construction project management. In February 2016, Ordone Co. welcomed a new partner, Ms. Sonji White. Ms. White, brings in 15+ years of invaluable project and construction management experience. Additionally, Ordone Co. has applied for the State & Local Disadvantaged Business Enterprise Certification as both a 100% minority and woman owned business.

CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT

We adhere to strict construction, quality control, and safety guidelines throughout the construction process in order to deliver a final product that satisfies the intended design.

Project Management

Our ability to efficiently and effectively manage every aspect of our client’s projects can be attributed directly to our commitment to project transparency. With clear milestones, deliverables, and accountability we empower both our staff and clients in every phase of work.

Grant Management

Our team has extensive experience in negotiating fair and reasonable awards for our clients.

Program Management

Understanding the client and their needs is essential to our success. We develop relationships with our team that enable us to assign, prioritize and accomplish their goals.

Principals

Courtney Ordone, President

Ms. Ordone’s combination of experience with FEMA and GOHSEP funding, city planning processes, project management, and historic construction give her the unique ability to manage projects from inception to reality. She is known for her ability to navigate difficult processes with ease while maintaining stellar relationships with all project team members.

She is an experienced Project Manager directly managing multiple public and private non-for profit facility projects valued at approximately $90 million dollars. She specializes in facilities funded by FEMA and State grant monies. Non-Profit clients included Touro Hospital, Jesuit HS, Ponchartrain Housing Corporation, and Xavier University.

Ms. Ordone led the coordination and negotiations in many FEMA PW claims negotiations for both the City of New Orleans and The Sulzer Group netting an additional 30+ million dollars in increased funding for multiple city owned facilities and a variety of individual Private Non-Profit clients. While navigating grant processes she managed the design phase of various projects to ensure that the scope, schedule, budget and design conformed to the specialized requirements of each of the individual funding source. Also, facilitated, monitored, and mapped the planning, zoning and permitting processes for the City of New Orleans Hurricane Katrina Recovery Program thus expediting the schedules of numerous city facility projects.



Sonji White, Vice President

Ms. White is an experienced Project and Construction Manager qualified in the design oversite, estimating, bidding, scheduling, implementation, reporting and closeout of major commercial, educational and government construction projects.

Ms. White represented OPSB in strategic planning, design and construction/renovation of multiple schools with a combined budget of over $90 million. She gained invaluable knowledge of FEMA policies and procedures while managing the assessments of over 20 City of New Orleans buildings and parks. As an Owner’s representative for the City of New Orleans, Ms. White was integral in the City of New Orleans receiving over $80 million in FEMA funding for the replacement of city owned buildings damaged during Hurricane Katrina.

As a result of her involvement in various projects throughout the city of New Orleans, Ms. White has gained knowledge in Historic renovation guidelines, lead and asbestos remediation regulations, and the CNO comprehensive zoning ordinance. Experience as an Owners’ Representative for several entities has provided her the relationship skills to successfully coordinate across the table between the owner, end user, designer and contractor. Besides her proven leadership ability, Ms. White has extensive experience in large capital improvement programs in both California and Louisiana.
