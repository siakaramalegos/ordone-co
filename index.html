Bringing Properties Back to Life

The Ordone Company is a private real estate investment firm specializing in the rehabilitation of distressed and blighted residential property. Our mission is simple – to bring communities back to life.

We meticulously renovate each property, mixing original design elements with modern functionality so that our properties meet the high standards of today’s buyers while contributing toward community recovery.

Acquisition & Selling

We research and focus on specific communities with the highest need for redevelopment to better locate high-potential properties.

Project Management

With our extensive experience, our team keeps track of all the moving pieces to stay on target for on-time completion.

Financial Management

Our in-depth financial analysis of every project ensures the highest possible returns to our investors.

Custom Design

We custom design floorplans, layouts, and fixture selection with care resulting in beautiful exterior and interior details.

Courtney Ordone

Founder & CEO

About

Ordone Company was founded by New Orleans native Courtney Ordone. She graduated from Tulane University’s Business School and was on the project management team for the City of New Orleans Katrina Recovery Program.

Since Hurricane Katrina, Courtney has personally redeveloped property all over New Orleans, making her one of the most trusted and knowledgeable real estate renovation consultants in the area.
