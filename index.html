Keeping It Real Estate

Ordone Company is a premier, privately owned real estate investment firm that strives to create lasting value for our investors, build community pride, and provide equity to everyone that purchases an Ordone Co. property.

At Ordone Company, we apply our strengths as a leading and renovation firm to deliver beautiful homes, unlock community equity, tackle urban blight, and propel our growth. Our capital fuels the redevelopment of neglected properties in both emerging and established urban communities. Our priority is to infuse equity each property thus creating community wealth. This commitment is reflected in our meticulous renovations, professionalism, and passion for excellence.

About

Courtney Ordone

Founder & CEO

The firm was founded in 2013 by Courtney Ordone, our President & Chief Executive Officer. Ordone Co. began with the goal of becoming a leading real estate investment business built on a foundation of uncompromising commitment to excellence. Ms. Ordone’s vision was to create an independently owned and operated real estate investment firm that put clients’ needs first. Ordone Co. is committed providing long lasting value to each community that it invests and to adhere to the highest ethical standards in order to create long-term value for all stakeholders.

Ms. Ordone was on the project management team for the City of New Orleans Katrina Recovery Program. Since Hurricane Katrina, she has personally redeveloped property all over New Orleans, making her one of the most trusted and knowledgeable real estate renovation consultants in the area. Ms. Ordone graduated from Tulane University’s Business School.
